Priority Triage: Lead Flow Risks

What's actively losing leads vs. what can wait for the React rebuild.

Date: 2 April 2026 Context: React migration planned Incident: 80+ leads lost, $50k+ revenue impact

Recent Incident — Why This Matters

On 1-2 April 2026, 80+ leads were found lost due to CRS development work:

• 88 leads had email address stored as first name in HubSpot (field mapping bug)

• Refinance quiz webhook was never connected — leads not captured at all

• 30+ leads lost in March alone, some dating back to October 2025

• Estimated $50,000+ revenue loss — leads not contacted within 24 hours go to competitors

The forms showed success messages to users. The leads just never arrived in HubSpot. Silent failure is the worst kind of failure.

P0 — Leads Being Lost (Fix Now)

P0-1: Verify ALL form submissions reach HubSpot after CRS changes

What happened: CRS dev work broke field mappings (email as first name) and left the refinance webhook disconnected. 80+ leads silently lost.

Risk: Other forms may have the same issue. We haven't verified every form end-to-end since the CRS changes.

Action: Run dry-run E2E tests on every lead capture form. Submit test data → verify HubSpot contact appears → verify field mapping is correct (name is name, email is email, phone is phone).

Test: npx playwright test --grep @dry-run (existing tests for F-01, F-02). Need to add dry-runs for F-04, F-07, F-09.

Acceptance: Every form produces a correctly-mapped HubSpot contact within 5 minutes of submission.

P0-2: Refinance quiz on /lower-your-rate/ — quiz data silently discarded

What: CF7 #14462 captures name/email/phone but all 5 quiz steps (interest rate, loan amount, term, priority) are thrown away. 12+ "empty" submissions in HubSpot.

Revenue impact: These leads arrive with zero context. Broker has no idea what rate they're on or how much they owe — has to ask everything from scratch on the call, reducing close rate.

Fix: Add hidden fields to CF7 #14462 for quiz answers. JS populates them before submit. See full fix spec.

Acceptance: Submit refinance quiz → HubSpot contact has interest rate, loan amount, term, and priority fields populated.

P0-3: Homepage quiz submitToContactForm7() sends refinance fields as dashes

What: The homepage quiz already sends mortgage-rate, still-owe, loan-term, important to CF7 #72896 — but hardcoded to "-". Even when a user goes through the purchase flow and these fields could have values, they're dashes.

Fix: Populate these fields from the answers object instead of hardcoding dashes.

Acceptance: Submit homepage quiz → HubSpot contact has actual quiz answers, not dashes.

The #1 Rule for React Launch

Never show a success message unless you've confirmed the data reached HubSpot.

The WordPress site shows "Thank you! We'll be in touch" the moment CF7 accepts the POST — even if HubSpot tracking is broken and the lead is lost. The React site must verify the submission reached HubSpot (or at minimum, a backend endpoint that guarantees delivery) before showing success.
